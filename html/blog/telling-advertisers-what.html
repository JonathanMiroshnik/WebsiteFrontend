Advertisers, let me tell you what I want

Today I used social media. A mistake was clearly made, yes, but still I picked up the phone and clicked into the app, and what I found were posts, and I was having fun, but suddenly a video advertisement popped into my view, trying to sell me something I was obviously not going to buy, and I thought to myself, why are they showing this to me?

Of course I could just:

  • Install an adblocker
  • Stop using social media
  • Do something more radical even still

But I wouldn't dare go through the front door on this one, instead I will try to help my people, the people who want nothing other than to sell me quality products for my own good.

And so, Advertisers, let me tell you what I want. There is no need for algorithms and behavioural analysis. No need for dirty tricks.

Give me an input field, I'll write exactly what I would like to see in my ads. This is a serious proposition.

I'm willing to bet there's a good chunk of people who would end up more useful to Adsense if it were to just listen to them.

I don't need another university degree right now, definitely not in that school, and I don't need that shampoo.

We all know this.

So, I'll be the first one to start.

THEORETICAL ADVERTISER INPUT FIELD
That's my order.
